Caxias3D is an interactive three-dimensional visualization application representing socioeconomic data from the neighborhoods of Caxias do Sul (RS, Brazil) based on the 2022 IBGE Census. The application allows users to explore indicators of average income, demographic density, literacy rates, and formal employment by city region, using territorial boundaries defined by Municipal Law 8.741/2021.

The project combines open government data with geovisualization technologies, functioning as an analytical tool for researchers, public managers, and citizens interested in the city's territorial dynamics. It integrates the research line on complex systems and territory developed within the doctoral program in Administration (PPGA/UCS).

Access: [caxias3d.netlify.app](https://caxias3d.netlify.app)

Sources: IBGE Census 2022; Municipality of Caxias do Sul (Law 8.741/2021); OpenStreetMap/Nominatim
