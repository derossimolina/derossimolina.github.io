This systematic literature review maps computational methods used for identifying money laundering patterns by criminal organizations. The protocol, developed in accordance with PRISMA 2020 guidelines, has been registered on Zenodo with a permanent DOI and uses ASReview software for AI-assisted abstract screening. The protocol repository is publicly available on GitHub/Zenodo, ensuring methodological reproducibility and transparency.

This research is part of the doctoral dissertation project at PPGA/UCS (Class 2026), supervised by Prof. Dr. Mateus Panizzon and co-supervised by Prof. Dr. Rafael Perini, funded by PROSUC/CAPES (Modality I).

Co-authors: Mateus Panizzon; Rafael Perini
