## Doctoral Thesis

My research investigates the application of **digital twins** in the study and prevention of urban crime. The proposal combines computational simulation models with spatial and criminological data to support public security policies.

**Program:** PPGA — University of Caxias do Sul  
**Field:** Public Administration / Complex Systems

## Topics of interest

- Digital twins and urban simulation
- Computational criminology
- Complex adaptive systems
- Evidence-based public policy
